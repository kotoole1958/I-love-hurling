Why Hurling is so great

Speed of game

Hurling is reckoned to be one of the fstest field games in the world. The sliothar (ball) when struck acn travel up to 60 yds, suddenly turning defence into attack.

Strength of players

Hurling is a very physical game and hurleys can be lethal and dangerous weapons in the hands of a player with bad intent. It is hard to believe tht a few decdes ago, helmets were not even worn by players.

Competition

There are a number of teams able to compete at the highest level which gives close exciting encounters. It is difficult to fathom why ther are so many draws when a score can be achieved from 60 yds from goal and a team averages 25 scores a match.

Hand Eye Coordination

The hand eye coordination has to be developed from an early age and is is rare indeeed that it can be learned at a later age. Hence, why so many counties find it so difficult to compete at the highest level.
